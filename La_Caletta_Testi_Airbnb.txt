Residence La Caletta is located at Capo Piccolo, the headland where the Ionian Sea and the Gulf of Squillace meet, a few kilometres from Crotone (KR). The area is dominated by the Capo Rizzuto Marine Protected Area — the largest in Italy — with crystal-clear waters, rich posidonia seabeds and unspoilt natural scenery.

Immediate surroundings (0–5 km):
- Praialonga beach (150 m on foot, cliff and sand)
- Sovereto beach and Le Castella (medieval village with Aragonese castle on the sea, 5 km)
- Capo Colonna columns (Greek-Roman sanctuary, 5 km)
- Supermarket, pharmacy, bars and restaurants in Le Castella (5 km)

Recommended day trips (30–60 min):
- Crotone historic centre and National Archaeological Museum (20 km)
- Capo Rizzuto Island (snorkelling and diving)
- Sila National Park (mountain plateau, 80 km)
- Capo Vaticano and Costa degli Dei (Tyrrhenian Calabria, 2 h)
